Refund Policy

At Shopping App, we strive to provide the best shopping experience. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

Eligibility for Refunds

You may request a refund if:

  • The item is defective or damaged upon arrival.
  • You received the wrong item.
  • The item does not match the description on our website.

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@shoppingapp.com within 7 days of receiving the item.
  2. Provide your order number and a clear description of the issue.
  3. If applicable, attach images of the defective or incorrect item.
  4. Our team will review your request and respond within 48 hours.

Refund Method

If approved, refunds will be issued through the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

Certain items are not eligible for refunds, including:

  • Gift cards and digital products.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale."
  • Used or damaged items (not due to transit issues).

Contact Us

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team at support@shoppingapp.com.
